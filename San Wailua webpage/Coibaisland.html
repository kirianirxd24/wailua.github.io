Overview Itinerary

Itinerary⋮

Day 1

•Early in the morning, depart Santa Catalina.
•Lodging in the cabins
•Enjoy an afternoon snorkel right from our own beach with the potential to see rays, sharks, turtles and puffer fish.
•Enjoy a quiet evening on the beach with a campfire and unrestricted stargazing.

Day 2

•Visit the Howler Monkeys, black hawks, and osprey at Mango Beach, where you may beachcomb for buried riches.
•Explore the varied species while snorkeling in Isla Granito de Oro.
•Enjoy thermal waters from the Media Luna volcano.
•Pass a mangrove swamp on a kayak.
•Sightseeing and hiking along a trail in the temperate rainforest of Coiba.
•Snorkeling in Isla Granito de Oro, sighting of colored fish, with sea turtles and many more.
